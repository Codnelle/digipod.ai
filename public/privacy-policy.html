Website: www.digipod.tech
Product: Digipod (AI-Powered Admin Elimination Platform for Creative Agencies)

1. Who We Are

Digipod is an AI-powered platform designed to help creative agencies eliminate admin work and streamline client management. Our tools integrate with services like Gmail to automate emails, replies, and project communication.

2. What Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide
Account Information: Name, email, agency name, password
Billing Information: If you're on a paid plan, we collect payment data via our third-party processor (e.g., Stripe)
Client Data: Names, emails, and communication relevant to your agency's clients

b. Information We Access via Integrations
Gmail Access: If you connect your Gmail, we only access metadata and message content necessary to generate replies and manage client threads
Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance: We comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

c. Automatically Collected
Usage data: Log data, feature usage, error logs
Device data: Browser, operating system, IP address

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our platform
  • To automate and draft email responses using AI
  • To offer analytics and insights about your client communication
  • To personalize your experience and simplify admin tasks
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

  • We use end-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • All emails and client information are stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
  • Access is restricted internally on a need-to-know basis
  • We host data on secure, compliant cloud infrastructure (e.g., Google Cloud, AWS)
